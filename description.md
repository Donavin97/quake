## Key Features
- **Smart Alerts**: Get notified when earthquakes match your criteria. Create multiple profiles with custom locations, magnitude thresholds, and quiet hours. Critical alerts can bypass Do Not Disturb, with distinct sounds for major quakes (6.0+)
- **Explore Seismic Activity**: View earthquakes on an interactive map or sorted list. Filter by magnitude, location, and time
- **Seismograph Viewer**: View real waveform data from nearby stations. Listen to seismic data as audio with our sonification feature. Pinch to zoom, drag to pan - the red marker shows when the quake occurred
- **Personalize**: Set your timezone for accurate quiet hours. Choose light, dark, or system theme. Select your preferred data source (USGS, EMSC, or SEC). Report if you felt a quake
