# QuakeTrack - Real-time Alerts

**Short Description (74 chars):** Real-time earthquake alerts, interactive maps, and seismic waveform viewer.

## Long Description

Stay informed and safe with QuakeTrack, your comprehensive companion for real-time earthquake monitoring worldwide. Designed for both casual users and seismic enthusiasts, QuakeTrack provides accurate data, advanced alerting, and unique visualization tools in one sleek, modern interface.

### Key Features

*   **Real-time Global Data:** Instant access to earthquake data from major global sources including USGS, EMSC, and SeisComP (SEC). Stay updated with the latest seismic events as they happen.
*   **Intelligent Notification Profiles:** Create multiple personalized alert profiles. Define specific geographic regions (radius around a point), magnitude thresholds, and custom quiet hours for each. QuakeTrack ensures you only get the alerts that matter to you.
*   **Safety Circles:** Create private groups for family and friends. Share invite codes instantly, see everyone's safety status during an earthquake, and coordinate your response with one-tap status updates.
*   **Community Seismograph:** Help scientific detection by contributing anonymous seismic data while your phone is charging. View your personal contribution history, see when your device's readings were confirmed by the network, and receive named community alerts before official data arrives.
*   **Advanced Vibration Customization:** Customize the tactile feedback for seismograph operations. Adjust duration and pulse patterns for success and error signals with built-in testing.
*   **Life-Safety Proximity Alerts:** Receive critical notifications for significant earthquakes occurring near your current physical location, even when the app is in the background.
*   **Interactive Seismograph & Sonification:** View real-time waveforms from nearby seismic stations. Experience the earth's movement through our unique "sonification" feature, which converts seismic data into audible sound.
*   **Seismic Trends & Statistics:** Visualize earthquake activity with interactive charts and 7-day trends. Understand magnitude distributions and frequency across different regions at a glance.
*   **Lithosphere Design System:** A beautiful, high-contrast interface featuring "Obsidian Dark" and "Basalt Light" themes, optimized for readability in all lighting conditions.
*   **Global Archive Search:** Explore years of seismic history. Search the global database by location name, magnitude, or date to understand regional activity trends.
*   **Emergency Safety Toolbox:** Access offline-ready preparedness guides, emergency kit checklists, and dynamic local emergency numbers that update based on your current country.
*   **Interactive Map Overlays:** Visualize tectonic plate boundaries, fault lines, and theoretical felt-radius circles to better understand the impact and context of every quake.
*   **"I Felt It" Reporting:** Contribute to community science by reporting your own observations. View a "felt intensity" map generated by other users in real-time.
*   **Dynamic App Shortcuts:** Quickly jump to the Map, the latest Major Quake, or the Safety Toolbox directly from your home screen.
*   **Multilingual Support:** Fully localized for English, Turkish, and Spanish.

Whether you're in a high-activity zone or just curious about the world's seismic activity, QuakeTrack provides the precision and tools you need to stay connected and prepared.
