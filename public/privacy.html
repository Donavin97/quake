Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

QuakeTrack ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By downloading and using QuakeTrack, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address and name (if provided) through Firebase Authentication
  • Location Data: We collect your device's location to create notification profiles for earthquake alerts near specific locations
  • User Content: Felt reports you submit, including your location and experience description

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App interactions, features used, crash reports

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide earthquake alerts and notifications based on your custom profiles
  • Display earthquake data on interactive maps
  • Authenticate your account
  • Improve and optimize our app
  • Show advertisements through Google AdMob

Third-Party Services

QuakeTrack uses the following third-party services:

Firebase (Google)

  • Firebase Authentication: Manages user accounts
  • Cloud Firestore: Stores user profiles, notification profiles, and felt reports
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: Sends push notifications
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Reports app crashes

Google

  • Google Mobile Ads: Displays advertisements
  • Google Sign-In: Alternative authentication method

Data Providers

  • USGS, EMSC, and SEC: Provide earthquake data (we forward your location only when you request custom alerts)

Data Storage & Security

Your personal data is stored on Firebase's servers. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data:

  • With third-party service providers who assist us
  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights or safety

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out: Disable location services or push notifications in device settings

To request data deletion, contact us through the app or via our website.

Advertisements

We show advertisements through Google AdMob, which may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

Cookies

We do not use cookies directly. However, third-party services (including Google AdMob and Firebase) may use cookies and similar technologies.

Children's Privacy

QuakeTrack is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.
